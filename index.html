Server Features

A list of our selected features.

What do we have to offer?

Experience MapleStory like never before with heaps of fun and engaging features found exclusively on MaplePlace.

Low Rates

With 2/1/1 rates, MaplePlace provides an exciting and challenging twist to keep the adventure going!

Revamped HP System

Tired of playing servers that require HP Washing? Good! On MaplePlace, you won't have to HP wash ever again!

Skill Rebalance

We are constantly working to make all classes viable, relevant and enjoyable as possible with our own unique changes to certain skills and mechanics.

Live Restarts

No more old-fashioned server restarts. When MaplePlace restarts, you will not be forced out of the game!

Anti-leech System

MaplePlace discourages players from buying leech from other players in order to promote party-play.

Unique Systems

MaplePlace offers you a set of unique systems in place to enhance your gameplay, and more to come! For further information about our unique systems click here.
